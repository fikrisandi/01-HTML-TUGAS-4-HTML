Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the world stage for 16 straight years-the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been-on paper-Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consident challenger.
